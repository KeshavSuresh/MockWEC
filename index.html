About WEC

The largest engineering competition at Canada's #1 engineering school. Two thrilling days. Six categories. One unforgettable weekend of designing, debating, and dreaming up what's next.

Competition Categories

Pick your battlefield. Each track is judged independently by industry experts.

🛠️

Junior Design

Hands-on prototyping for 1A & 1B students. Build a working solution to a surprise prompt in 6 hours.

Teams of 4
🧠

Senior Design

For upper-year engineers. Tackle an open-ended technical challenge with real-world constraints.

Teams of 4
📊

Consulting

Crack a business case for a real company and present your strategy to a panel of executives.

Teams of 3
💻

Programming

Competitive algorithms & data structures. Solve a graded set of problems against the clock.

Teams of 2
⚙️

Re-Engineering

Reverse-engineer an existing product and pitch a redesign that improves cost, sustainability, or UX.

Teams of 3
🧬

Bio-Engineering

Design a biomedical or biotech intervention. Judged on impact, feasibility, and ethics.

Teams of 3
